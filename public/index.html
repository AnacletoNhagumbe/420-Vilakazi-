Cannabis Products Overview

Strains

Weed strains are various types of cannabis plants with distinct characteristics. Each strain has unique effects, flavors, and potency.

Edibles

Edibles are cannabis-infused food products that provide a different experience compared to smoking or vaping. They come in many forms such as gummies, chocolates, and baked goods.

Shrooms

Shrooms, or psychedelic mushrooms, are fungi that contain psychoactive compounds. They are used for their mind-altering effects.

Extracts

Extracts are concentrated forms of cannabis that can include oils, tinctures, and concentrates. They offer potent effects and are used in various ways.
